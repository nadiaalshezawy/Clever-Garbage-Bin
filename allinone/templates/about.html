Brief Description:

The project is to have a smart garbage bin that have a barcode for each house and

when collected it will allow to measure the weight of garbage related to this house.

This information can be shared to the owner by application showing daily waste level

versus recycled garbe.


Our Vision:

Our vision is to have data about the waste and recycele garbage produced by individual

and help in reducig the waste level and achieve the UAE 2020 vision to increase the

recycled waste into 75% of total waste.


Our Mission:

  • Achieve a healthier environmentby reducing the waste level.

  • Provide the country with another source of income and clean energy.

  • Raise the awarness and responsibilty in the community.
